How to play UNI!

UNI! is a quick, free card game you play online with up to four people. If you've ever played a colour-and-number card game like UNO® or Crazy Eights, you already know the gist: be the first to get rid of all your cards. Never played one? Don't worry, you'll have it down in a couple of minutes.

Rules at a glance

  • Players: Up to 4 players online.
  • Goal: Be the first to empty your hand. Don't forget to call "UNI!" when you have one card left!
  • Start of round: Everyone is dealt 7 cards. The remaining cards form the draw pile, and the top card is flipped to start the discard pile.
  • On your turn: Match the top card of the discard pile by colour or number/symbol. If you can't, draw a card!
