How to play UNI!

UNI! is a quick, free card game you play online with up to four people. If you've ever played a colour-and-number card game like UNO® or Crazy Eights, you already know the gist: be the first to get rid of all your cards. Never played one? Don't worry, you'll have it down in a couple of minutes.

The goal

Empty your hand before anyone else does. Everyone is dealt 7 cards to start; the rest become the draw pile, and one card is turned face up to kick off the discard pile.

The cards

There are four colours in the deck:

  • Number cards (0–9) in red, yellow, blue and green.
  • Skip: the next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse: flips the direction of play.
  • Draw Two: the next player draws 3 cards and is skipped.
  • Jolly: play it on anything and pick the next colour.
  • Jolly Draw Four: pick the next colour, and the next player draws 4.

Taking your turn

Look at the top of the discard pile and play a card that matches its colour or its number/symbol. So on a red 7, you could drop any red card, or any colour's 7. A Jolly can go down at any time and lets you choose the colour that comes next.

Stuck with nothing to play? Draw a card from the pile. If it works, you can play it right away; if not, your turn's over.

Calling "UNI!"

The moment you're down to a single card, shout UNI! (well, tap the button). If you forget, you'll have to draw a penalty!

Winning

Play your final card before everyone else and the round is yours.

Optional house rules

Hosting a lobby? You can switch on a few extra rules to shake things up. new fun rules are constantly being added!


That's everything. Jump into a game, or have a look at the FAQ first.
